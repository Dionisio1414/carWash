About
car wash Jack’s

At Jack’s Car Wash, we have been doing our best to provide our customers with the easiest, most efficient way to clean cars for over 30 years.

It all started when Jack & Debbie Bailey opened their first car wash in the 1980’s. They introduced the Foaming Brush to the area resulting in cleaner cars than ever before. Expanding to nearby towns, 6 additional locations were built across the Texas Panhandle. Today with 4 locations, 2 in Dalhart and 2 in Dumas, we continue to offer the latest in technology with our products and equipment.

We are proudly still a family owned and operated company, including 4 generations that continue to keep this company on the right path of offering our customers quality and convenience.

Jack’s Car Wash is thankful for our employees and a community that we will continue to serve with the latest in car care.
